About Beak-a-Boo: A JA Manitoba Company

**Mission:** To inspire the next generation of Manitoba birders by providing an accessible, engaging, and localized resource for avian identification and conservation awareness.

The JA Story

Beak-a-Boo was founded in 2025 as part of the **Junior Achievement (JA) of Manitoba** Company Program by a team of Grade 12 students from **Windsor Park Collegiate** in Winnipeg. Recognizing Manitoba's incredible role in the North American flyway, our student-led business saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between digital content and local wildlife education. This project is driven by entrepreneurship, technology, and a deep appreciation for the natural world.

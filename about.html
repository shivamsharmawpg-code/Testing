About Beak-a-Boo: A JA Manitoba Company

**Mission:** To connect our community with Manitoba's incredible avian biodiversity while providing practical tools and education through our student-developed **Bird Watching Kit**.

The JA Story & Our Product

Beak-a-Boo was founded in 2025 as part of the **Junior Achievement (JA) of Manitoba** Company Program by a diverse team of students spanning Grade 9 through 12 at **Windsor Park Collegiate** in Winnipeg. Our student-led business focuses on creating educational tools. Our primary product is a curated **Bird Watching Kit**, designed to give beginners everything they need to start birding, with proceeds funding future educational initiatives and local conservation efforts.
